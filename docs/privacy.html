Privacy Policy

InTheLoop by AutoTok is a local-first desktop and command-line workflow for generating, reviewing, and preparing short-form TikTok video packages. This policy explains how information is handled when an operator uses InTheLoop.

Information Processed Locally

InTheLoop may process source text, generated scripts, narration audio, subtitles, background media, rendered videos, review state, publication records, and local analytics records in the operator's configured local data directory.

TikTok Account Authorization

InTheLoop does not connect to TikTok accounts, request TikTok OAuth scopes, or store TikTok access tokens for publishing. The public website does not collect or store TikTok tokens.

Publication Data

InTheLoop prepares local upload files only. Video files and captions are sent to TikTok only if the operator manually uploads them through TikTok's own app or website. TikTok's processing of submitted content is governed by TikTok's own terms and privacy policies.

Website Data

This public website is informational. It does not include account creation, payment processing, newsletter signup, tracking pixels, or analytics collection controlled by InTheLoop.

No Sale of Personal Information

InTheLoop does not sell personal information.

Data Retention

Local project artifacts remain on the operator's machine until the operator deletes them or applies local retention procedures. Publicly posted content remains subject to the destination platform's controls.

Contact

For privacy questions about this project, contact the repository owner through the GitHub repository associated with InTheLoop by AutoTok.
