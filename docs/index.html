Local-first video workflow

InTheLoop helps operators create, review, and prepare TikTok-ready short-form videos.

InTheLoop is powered by AutoTok, a desktop and command-line workflow for turning approved source text and authorized media into reviewable vertical video packages.

Create Locally

Import approved story text, narration audio, subtitles, and authorized background media into a local workspace.

Review First

Preview the generated video package locally and approve it before preparing a publishing handoff.

Publish Manually

Use the generated TikTok upload package, then manually upload and publish from your own TikTok account.

TikTok Manual Handoff

InTheLoop prepares a local folder containing the rendered MP4, suggested caption, metadata, and upload instructions. The account owner reviews TikTok's upload screen and clicks the final publish button manually.

  • No TikTok Login Kit, OAuth scopes, Content Posting API, Direct Post, or API tokens are used for publishing.
  • Generated content stays local unless the operator manually uploads it to TikTok.
  • The app does not automate comments, direct messages, follows, likes, or other engagement behavior.

Operator Responsibilities

Operators are responsible for using source text, audio, and media they own or are authorized to use. InTheLoop does not provide legal advice, guarantee reach, or determine whether specific content is eligible for monetization or platform distribution.
